Tackling Gender-Based Violence and Promoting Economic Empowerment for Women and Young Girls.

Based in Dar Es Salaam, we champion transformative gender approaches, advocating for grassroots empowerment. Through lobbying, advocacy, and skills development, we enable women to participate in socio-economic activities and decision-making. Together, we build a brighter, equal future.

  • Dedicated to tackling gender-based violence faced by women and young girls.
  • Empowers women at the grassroots level to engage in economic activities and decision making.
  • Implements transformative gender approaches for sustainable change.
  • Advocates for right-based frameworks and skills development to promote positive social and economic impact.

Together, we can make a lasting impact for women's economic empowerment and meaningful participation in decision-making.

Our Story

At Nyayo Tanzania, we are dedicated to fostering gender equality and empowerment for women and young girls. Through our comprehensive programs and initiatives, we tackle gender-based violence, promote economic empowerment, and advocate for women's rights. Our work encompasses grassroots interventions, skills development, and advocacy for transformative change. Together, we create a more inclusive and equitable society.

What we do

Tackling gender-based violence

Nyayo Tanzania works to address and eliminate various forms of gender-based violence faced by women and young girls, particularly when they engage in economic activities and decision-making processes.

Promoting high-quality education and infrastructure

The organization strives to promote and improve the quality of education and infrastructure to empower women and girls.

Enhancing vocational skills and entrepreneurship

Nyayo Tanzania focuses on training and enhancing vocational skills, entrepreneurship, and record-keeping to equip women and young girls with the necessary tools for economic empowerment.

Advocating against gender-based violence

The organization actively engages in advocacy efforts to raise awareness, promote policies, and advocate for the rights of women and girls, aiming to prevent and combat gender-based violence.

Supporting marginalized and underprivileged groups

Nyayo Tanzania is committed to supporting vulnerable, marginalized, voiceless, underprivileged, or disadvantaged groups, with a specific focus on women, young girls, and children.

Conflict resolution and dispute settlement

The organization plays a role in conflict resolution, providing a mechanism for settling disputes and conflicts that may arise within the organization.
